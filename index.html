About Me

I am a PhD candidate who investigates how regulatory information is encoded into the genome using a combination of machine learning and evolutionary biology.

Currently, I am a member of the Mitchell and Moses labs at the University of Toronto. Before starting my PhD, I was a software developer at the Ontario Institute for Cancer Research (OICR). Prior to my work at OICR, I completed my Bachelor of Computer Science, Honours Bioinformatics at the University of Waterloo.
